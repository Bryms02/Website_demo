Traveling to Taniti

Air

  • Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes.
  • The airport is currently being expanded to accommodate larger jets.

Sea

  • A small cruise ship is available to take to Taniti. It docks one night per week in Yellow Leaf Bay.

Getting Around Taniti

Rental Cars

  • Rental cars can be rented from a local rental agency near the airport

Bus

  • Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m.
  • Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxi

  • Taxis are available in Taniti City.

Bicycle Rental

  • Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors.
  • Helmets are required by law.
